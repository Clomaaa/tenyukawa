What's our take on this?


||

            In our opinion, the government and the people must join forces to solve climate change. They will manage the finances and the order of things to do while we follow and must interact with the community and cooperate in addressing the problems of climate change. Citizens must always remember that it is not only the government that will take action to be prepared and alert to the ongoing climate change because it is only right that the people unite in the programs or announcements to solve the problem of our climate. We also want to emphasize the other factors of climate change that can also give fear to the majority, so we should not be ashamed to discover other knowledge about the various effects of climate change.

            Furthermore, through our research, we will know the different aspects related to climate change such as its influence on us, why it happens, how it happens, what has been here since then and until now. Which can better understand and determine its value then especially in the present time. As we process the knowledge we really reflect and we imagine the reality that it is really hard to find a solution to what is happening when a lack of discipline and unity prevails. As students, we are doing our best to raise the hope that this problem can be solved immediately because when it gets worse in the coming years it will surely be more difficult for the government and the world to face the challenge brought about by climate change.

            As a whole, we want everyone to have a common goal for the good of each of our societies and we want to encourage everyone to be careful especially with the current pandemic. Be prepared for the next policies and protocols that will be presented to the people from superiors but we must still have our own duty to expedite action in these events. We believe that this can be a step towards unifying the goals that will be the key to solving these problems so we will continue to participate in programs that will be promoted depending on the state of our climate in which we are taking action.
